At Braveheart Smoke Shop, we're proud to be located on the Poospatuck Indian Reservation in Mastic, NY. We specialize in providing high-quality smoking products and accessories to satisfy all your 420 needs.

Our shop is run by a team of knowledgeable and friendly staff who are passionate about the culture and community we serve. We're committed to providing our customers with a welcoming environment where they can feel comfortable browsing our selection and asking any questions they may have.
